Tax-Analysts


During my exploration of n8n, I created a workflow as a learning exercise to model a real-world government process related to income tax filing.
The workflow was designed to mimic the scenario faced by many individuals during that time. As a means of improving my understanding of complex processes in n8n, I executed it successfully. Recently, I revisited this workflow and updated it using dummy data in Supabase,
generating an SQL schema for cleanliness and presentability. By refreshing the data and executing the tables, I aimed to create a more polished representation of the original logic while maintaining its integrity. 
This exercise allowed me to reinforce my understanding of n8n's capabilities in modeling real-world processes, especially those with a governmental context.
I am eager to apply this knowledge and continue learning about n8n to improve my problem-solving skills and contribute effectively to future projects.
